Europe & the Middle East

The Europe and Middle East Region spans the EurAsian Continent; from the picturesque streets of Lisbon in the west to the breathtaking wilderness of Kamchatka in Russia's far east. This is the mission of the Europe and Middle East United Pentecostal Church: to share the good news of a powerful Savior.
